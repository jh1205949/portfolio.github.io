Birds of Prey

This website will have information on Birds of Prey. Birds of prey can be found all over the world. their are over 560 species of bird of prey including falcons, red kites, osprey, California Condor, and much more .

Hear are some facts on Birds of prey

  1. the Andean condor is the largest bird of prey, With a maximum wingspan of 10 ft 10 in and a maximum weight of 33 lb.
  2. Eagles have 4-5x better vision than humans
  3. Hawks flying speed is 20-40 m.p.h and can reach up to 120 m.p.h when diving
